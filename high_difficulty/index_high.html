Minesweepers

Goal of the game:

  • Clicking on squares will uncover them.
  • An uncovered square will show either a number or a mine.
  • A number in a square tells you how many mines are adjacent to that square.
  • If you click on a mine you lose.
  • Uncover all the unmined squares to win!

Controls:

  • Right-click on your mouse to uncover the squares.
  • Left-click to mark the square with red flag.
